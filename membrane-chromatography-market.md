<p><p>Membrane chromatography is a type of chromatography that uses membranes as the stationary phase. There are different types of membrane chromatography including Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography, Affinity Film Chromatography, and Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography. Ion Exchange Membrane Chromatography separates molecules based on their charge, Affinity Film Chromatography separates molecules based on specific interactions, and Hydrophobic Interaction Membrane Chromatography separates molecules based on their hydrophobicity. These different types of membrane chromatography offer unique advantages and can be used in various applications within the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
