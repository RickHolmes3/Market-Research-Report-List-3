<p><p>Airport explosives detectors come in two main types: portable and fixed. Portable detectors are handheld devices that can be easily moved around and used in different areas of the airport. They are typically used for spot-checking suspicious items or individuals. Fixed detectors, on the other hand, are stationary units installed at key points throughout the airport, such as baggage screening areas or entrances. These detectors continuously monitor for explosives and other dangerous substances, providing a more comprehensive security solution. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining airport safety and preventing potential threats.</p></p>
