<p><p>The Foodborne Pathogen Detection Kit market includes various types such as PCR and ELISA. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a technique used to amplify specific DNA sequences, allowing for the identification of foodborne pathogens. PCR kits are widely used for their accuracy and sensitivity in identifying pathogens. Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) kits detect the presence of foodborne pathogens by measuring the reactions between antigens and antibodies. ELISA kits are highly sensitive and can quickly identify pathogens, making them a preferred choice in food safety laboratories.</p></p>
