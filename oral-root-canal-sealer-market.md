<p><p>Oral root canal sealants are used in dentistry to fill and seal the root canal after a root canal treatment. There are several types of sealants available in the market. Resin sealants use synthetic resins to provide a strong and durable seal. ZOE sealants use zinc oxide and eugenol to create a temporary seal. Dioxide mineral aggregate sealants use a combination of minerals to provide a stable and long-lasting seal. Sealants containing bioceramics utilize bioactive materials to promote healing and regeneration. Encapsulants with glass ionomer provide a protective barrier and enhance adhesion. Sealants containing calcium hydroxide offer antimicrobial properties and stimulate tissue repair.</p></p>
