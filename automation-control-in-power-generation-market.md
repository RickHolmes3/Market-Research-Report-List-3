Distributed Control System (DCS) helps coordinate and control power plant operations, ensuring efficiency. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) enables monitoring and control of remote equipment and processes. Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) is used for process automation, ensuring precise and reliable control of various power generation components. Manufacturing Execution System (MES) streamlines operations by managing production processes, optimizing efficiency, and accurately tracking power generation metrics.</p></p>
