<p><p>Automotive 3-D HUD market types include combiner and windshield displays. Combiner HUDs project information onto a separate display surface in the driver's line of sight, typically a small clear screen above the dashboard. Windshield HUDs, on the other hand, project information directly onto the windshield, allowing the driver to see essential data without taking their eyes off the road. Both types of HUDs enhance driver safety by providing critical information in a convenient and unobtrusive manner.</p></p>
