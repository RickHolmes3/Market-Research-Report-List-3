Controlled Motorways utilize variable speed limits and ramp control signals to manage traffic. Dynamic Hard Shoulder Running Schemes involve opening the hard shoulder as an extra lane during peak hours. All Lane Running Schemes permanently convert the hard shoulder into a running lane, requiring emergency refuge areas. These market types aim to enhance capacity and efficiency on roads by implementing intelligent transportation systems and innovative road management strategies.</p></p>
