<p><p>The fetal electrocardiogram (ECG) monitor market is classified into two types: stationary and portable. The stationary fetal ECG monitors are typically larger devices that are fixed to a single location, such as a hospital or clinic. They offer advanced features and better recording accuracy but are less convenient for mobility. On the other hand, portable monitors are compact, lightweight devices that allow for easy transportation and monitoring of fetal heart activity at home or on the go. While they may have slightly lower recording accuracy, they provide greater convenience and flexibility for expecting parents.</p></p>
