The market is driven by several factors, including the increasing demand for biopharmaceuticals, rising adoption of single-use technologies, and the growing need for cost-effective and flexible manufacturing solutions. Filtration devices and sampling systems are used to separate and analyze components of the bioprocess. Bioreactors and fermenters are single-use vessels for growing and cultivating cells.
