<p><p>De-aromatic solvents can be categorized into Low Flash Point, Medium Flash Point, and High Flash Point markets based on their boiling points and flammability. Low Flash Point solvents have a boiling point below 100°C, making them highly flammable and suitable for quick-drying applications. Medium Flash Point solvents have a boiling point between 100-150°C, offering a balance between flammability and evaporation rate. High Flash Point solvents have a boiling point above 150°C, making them less flammable and suitable for slower-evaporating applications. Each market type serves different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
