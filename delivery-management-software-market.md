<p><p>Delivery management software is available in two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise software is installed, hosted, and maintained locally on the user's hardware, providing complete control over the software and data. However, it requires upfront investment and technical expertise for installation and maintenance. On the other hand, Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers scalability, flexibility, and easier maintenance, as all updates and backups are managed by the provider, while users can access it from anywhere with an internet connection.</p></p>
