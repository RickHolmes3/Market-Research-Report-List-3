<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Braided Packing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Square Braided</li><li>Braid Over Braid</li><li>Braid Over Core</li><li>Interbraid</li><li>Die Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Braided packing market types include square braided, braid over braid, braid over core, interbraid, and die form. Square braided packing is made from square-shaped yarns, providing a tight seal. Braid over braid packing consists of one braided material wrapped around another. Braid over core packing has a central core surrounded by braided material. Interbraid packing is created by interweaving different materials. Die form packing is molded or shaped into a specific form for easy installation. Each type offers unique sealing properties for various industrial applications.</p></p>
