<p><p>The ionizing radiation sterilization market comprises three main types: electron beams, X-rays, and gamma rays. Electron beams use accelerated electrons to disrupt the DNA structure, making it impossible for microorganisms to reproduce. X-rays work by emitting high-energy photons that penetrate packaging materials and destroy pathogenic microorganisms. Gamma rays, on the other hand, are produced by the radioactive decay of cobalt-60 and have high penetration power, killing microorganisms by damaging their DNA. These ionizing radiation sterilization methods are commonly used in industries such as healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and food to ensure product safety and eliminate pathogens.</p></p>
