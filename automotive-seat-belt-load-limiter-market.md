<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Belt Load Limiter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Digressive Load Limiters</li><li>Progressive Load Limiters</li><li>Adaptive/ Switchable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive seat belt load limiters are designed to reduce the impact of a collision on the chest and ribs by limiting the force applied by the seat belt on the occupant. There are three main types of load limiters: </p><p>1. Digressive load limiters, which offer a constant level of force limitation throughout the collision</p><p>2. Progressive load limiters, which gradually increase the force limitation as the collision intensity rises</p><p>3. Adaptive/switchable load limiters, which can adjust their level of force limitation based on the occupant's size, weight, or seating position.</p></p>
