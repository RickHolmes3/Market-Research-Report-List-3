<p><p>Reverse mortgage providers offer different market types, catering to the diverse needs of seniors. Home Equity Conversion Mortgages (HECMs) are insured by the federal government and offer the most flexibility, allowing borrowers to choose among various payment options. Single-purpose reverse mortgages, provided by state and local governments and nonprofit organizations, are designed for specific purposes such as home repairs. Proprietary reverse mortgages are privately backed loans that cater to individuals with higher home values and typically offer larger loan amounts but with fewer restrictions than HECMs.</p></p>
