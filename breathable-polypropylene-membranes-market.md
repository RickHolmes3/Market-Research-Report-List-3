<p><p>Breathable polypropylene membranes are categorized into different types based on their structure and composition. Monolayer membranes consist of a single layer of polypropylene material, offering basic protection against moisture. Double layer membranes have an additional layer for improved breathability and durability. Three-layer membranes are further enhanced with an extra layer to provide superior waterproofing and breathability. Other types of membranes may include variations in layer composition or special coatings for specific applications. Each type offers varying levels of performance and protection for different needs.</p></p>
