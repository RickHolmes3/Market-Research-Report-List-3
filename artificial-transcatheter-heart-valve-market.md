<p><p>The Artificial Transcatheter Heart Valve market consists of three main types: Aortic Heart Valve, Mitral Heart Valve, and Tricuspid Heart Valve. Aortic Heart Valves are designed to replace the damaged valves in the aorta, which carries oxygen-rich blood from the heart to the rest of the body. Mitral Heart Valves are used to replace or repair the valves that separate the upper and lower chambers of the heart. Tricuspid Heart Valves provide a solution for replacing or repairing the valves between the right atrium and right ventricle of the heart.</p></p>
