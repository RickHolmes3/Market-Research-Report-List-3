<p><p>IWMS (Integrated Workplace Management System) software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. On the other hand, in the on-premises market, the software is installed and managed on the organization's own servers, providing more control and customization options but requiring internal IT resources for maintenance and upgrades.
