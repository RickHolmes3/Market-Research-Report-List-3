<p><p>The aircraft audio panel market consists of two main types - embedded type and portable type. Embedded type audio panels are permanently installed in the aircraft's cockpit and are integrated into the aircraft's avionics system. Portable type audio panels are standalone devices that can be easily moved between different aircraft. While embedded type panels offer seamless integration and functionality, portable type panels provide flexibility and convenience for users who may operate multiple aircraft. Both types cater to different needs within the aviation industry.</p></p>
