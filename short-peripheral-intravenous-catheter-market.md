<p><ul><li>Ported Short PIVC</li><li>Non-Ported Short PIVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheter (PIVC) market encompasses two main types: Ported Short PIVC and Non-Ported Short PIVC. Ported Short PIVCs are equipped with additional access ports, allowing healthcare professionals to administer multiple medications or fluids simultaneously. On the other hand, Non-Ported Short PIVCs lack these additional ports, limiting the administration to a single medication or fluid at a time. Both types serve the purpose of intravenous therapy but offer different capabilities depending on the medical requirements.</p></p>
