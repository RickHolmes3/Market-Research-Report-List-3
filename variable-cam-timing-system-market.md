<p><p>Variable Cam Timing System is available in two market types: Double and Single. In a Double market system, both the intake and exhaust camshafts are independently controlled, allowing for more precise control over valve timing for improved performance and efficiency. Single market systems, on the other hand, have only one camshaft that is adjustable, offering a simpler and more cost-effective solution for variable valve timing. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
