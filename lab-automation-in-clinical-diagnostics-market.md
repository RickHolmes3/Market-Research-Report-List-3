Robotic arms perform complex tasks like sample preparation and analysis. Automated storage and retrieval systems ensure efficient storage and retrieval of samples and reagents. Other types of lab automation systems may include instruments for sample processing, data management software, and robotic workstations for various lab tasks.</p></p>
