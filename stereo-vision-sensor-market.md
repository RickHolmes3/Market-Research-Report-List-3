<p><p>Stereo vision sensors are used in various applications such as robotics, autonomous vehicles, and industrial automation. There are two main types of stereo vision sensors: monocular and binocular. Monocular sensors use a single camera to capture images and determine depth perception through algorithms, while binocular sensors use two cameras to simulate human-like vision. Each type has its advantages and limitations, with monocular sensors being more cost-effective and binocular sensors providing more accurate depth perception. Both play a crucial role in advancing technology in different industries.</p></p>
