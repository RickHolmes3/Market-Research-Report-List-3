<p><p>Rotating table machining centers are classified into two main types based on the orientation of the table: vertical and horizontal. Vertical machining centers have the table positioned vertically, allowing for easier chip removal and accommodating taller workpieces. Horizontal machining centers have the table oriented horizontally, which is ideal for heavy-duty cutting and providing better stability for large workpieces. Both types offer different advantages and capabilities, catering to a wide range of industries and machining requirements.</p></p>
