<p><p>Wafer dicing blades are used to cut semiconductor wafers into individual chips during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of wafer dicing blades: hub dicing blades and hubless dicing blades. Hub dicing blades have a metal hub at the center to provide stability and precision during cutting. On the other hand, hubless dicing blades do not have a hub, allowing for more flexibility and faster cutting speeds. Both types of blades are essential tools in the semiconductor industry for achieving precise and accurate dicing results.</p></p>
