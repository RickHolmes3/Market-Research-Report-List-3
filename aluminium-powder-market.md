<p><ul><li>Non-Spherical Aluminum Powder</li><li>Spherical Aluminum Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium powder market is categorized into two types: non-spherical aluminium powder and spherical aluminium powder. Non-spherical aluminium powder refers to particles that have irregular shapes and varying sizes. This type of powder is commonly used in applications where high surface area is required, such as in the production of paints, pigments, and explosives. On the other hand, spherical aluminium powder consists of particles that have a uniform, round shape. It is commonly used in industries that require controlled flow and uniform packing, such as 3D printing, rocket propellants, and pyrotechnics.</p></p>
