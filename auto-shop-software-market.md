<p><p>Auto shop software is used by automotive repair shops to manage their day-to-day operations. There are two main types of auto shop software markets: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software operates via the internet, allowing users to access the system from any device with an internet connection. It offers flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, but requires a stable internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed and operated locally on the shop's computers. It offers more control over data and security, but requires technical expertise for maintenance and updates.</p></p>
