<p><strong>The Telescopic Laser Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1D</li><li>2D</li><li>3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Telescopic Laser Rangefinders are classified based on the number of dimensions they can measure. 1D rangefinders measure linear distance, such as the length of a room or the height of a building. 2D rangefinders measure both length and width, allowing for area calculations. 3D rangefinders measure length, width, and height, providing volumetric measurements. Each type has specific applications depending on the level of detail required for distance measurements, making them versatile tools for various industries such as construction, surveying, and forestry.</p></p>
