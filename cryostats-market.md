<p><strong>The Cryostats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bath Cryostats</li><li>Closed-Cycle Cryostats</li><li>Multistage Cryostats</li><li>Continuous-Flow Cryostats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryostats, which are used in various scientific and industrial applications, can be categorized into different types based on their operating principles. Bath cryostats use a liquid bath, such as liquid helium or nitrogen, to cool samples. Closed-cycle cryostats rely on a refrigeration system that circulates a refrigerant to provide cooling. Multistage cryostats use multiple stages of cooling, typically with different temperature ranges. Continuous-flow cryostats continuously replenish the cooling fluid in a closed loop system. These different types offer varying capabilities and temperature ranges to suit different experimental needs.</p></p>
