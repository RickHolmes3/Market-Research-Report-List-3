<p><p>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) equipment market includes two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO equipment and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO equipment. </p><p>VA ECMO equipment is used to provide both cardiac and respiratory support by oxygenating blood outside the body and returning it to the arterial system. </p><p>VV ECMO equipment, on the other hand, is primarily used to provide respiratory support by oxygenating blood outside the body and returning it to the venous system. Both types of ECMO equipment play a crucial role in providing life-saving support to patients with severe cardiac or respiratory failure.</p></p>
