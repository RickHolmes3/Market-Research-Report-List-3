<p><p>Corrugated container board market types refer to the different structures of cardboard used in packaging. A layer of cardboard consists of one single layer of corrugated material, providing basic protection. Two layers of cardboard have an additional layer of linerboard for added strength and durability. Three layer board features an extra layer of corrugated material between two layers of linerboard, offering the highest level of protection. Each type caters to different packaging needs based on the level of protection required.</p></p>
