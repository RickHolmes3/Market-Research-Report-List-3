<p><p>Parking lot sweepers are categorized into three main types: Mechanical Sweepers, Vacuum Air Sweepers, and Regenerative Air Sweepers. Mechanical sweepers use rotating brushes to sweep debris into a collection bin. Vacuum air sweepers use suction to collect debris from the surface. Regenerative air sweepers use a combination of suction and a high-velocity air stream to efficiently clean the parking lot. Each type of sweeper offers unique benefits and is suited for different types of cleaning tasks.</p></p>
