<p><p>Abrasives market consists of three main types -Bonded Abrasives, Coated Abrasives, and Super Abrasives. </p><p>- Bonded Abrasives are made by bonding abrasive grains together with various materials to form grinding wheels, blocks, or sticks.</p><p>- Coated Abrasives are abrasive grains coated on a backing material like paper or cloth to create sandpaper, belts, or discs.</p><p>- Super Abrasives are advanced materials with superior hardness and performance, used for precision grinding in industries like aerospace and automotive. Each type serves specific applications and industries within the abrasives market.</p></p>
