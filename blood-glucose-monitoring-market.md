<p><p>The blood glucose monitoring market includes two main types of devices: Single Point Blood Glucose Meters and Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems. Single Point Blood Glucose Meters are portable devices used to measure blood glucose levels at a specific moment in time. They require the user to prick their finger and apply a blood sample to a test strip. Continuous Glucose Monitoring Systems, on the other hand, provide real-time glucose readings throughout the day by inserting a small sensor under the skin. This allows for more frequent and accurate monitoring of glucose levels over a longer period of time.</p></p>
