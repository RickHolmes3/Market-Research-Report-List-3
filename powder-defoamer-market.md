<p><strong>The Powder Defoamer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder Defoamer Market Types, Type I and Type II, differ in their chemical composition and mode of action. Type I defoamers are silicone-based and work by breaking foam bubbles, while Type II defoamers are oil-based and function by lowering the surface tension of the foam. Type I defoamers are more effective in controlling foam in water-based systems, while Type II defoamers are preferred for oil-based systems. Both types play a crucial role in various industries to prevent foam formation and optimize production processes.</p></p>
