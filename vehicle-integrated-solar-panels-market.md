<p><p>Vehicle-integrated solar panels market consists of two main types - mono-crystalline silicon cells and multi-crystalline silicon cells. Mono-crystalline silicon cells are made from a single continuous crystal structure, making them more efficient and space-saving, but also more expensive. On the other hand, multi-crystalline silicon cells are made from multiple silicon crystals, which makes them slightly less efficient but more cost-effective. Both types of solar cells are used in the automotive industry to harness solar energy and power various vehicle systems.</p></p>
