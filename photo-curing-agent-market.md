<p><strong>The Photo Curing Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free-radical Type</li><li>Cationic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photo curing agents can be categorized into two main types: Free-radical Type and Cationic Type. </p><p>Free-radical Type curing agents involve the initiation of polymerization through the generation of free radicals upon exposure to light. This type is commonly used in applications such as adhesives, coatings, and inks.</p><p>On the other hand, Cationic Type curing agents involve the use of cations to initiate polymerization. This type is effective in applications where free-radical curing agents may not be suitable, such as in the curing of certain resins and coatings.</p></p>
