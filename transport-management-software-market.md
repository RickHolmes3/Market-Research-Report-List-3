<p><p>Transport management software is available in two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based transport management software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. This type offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for hardware installation and maintenance. On the other hand, on-premises transport management software is installed and operated on local servers within the organization's infrastructure. This type provides complete control and data security but requires dedicated IT resources and upfront investment for hardware and software installation.</p></p>
