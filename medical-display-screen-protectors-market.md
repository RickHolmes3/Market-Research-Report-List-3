<p><p>Medical display screen protectors are widely used in healthcare settings to protect screens of medical devices from scratches, damages, or contaminants. There are different types of screen protectors available in the market. PET (Polyethylene terephthalate) protectors offer high durability and clarity, providing effective protection against scratches. TPU (Thermoplastic polyurethane) protectors are highly flexible, with self-healing properties that can repair minor scratches. Other types of protectors include anti-glare, anti-microbial, and privacy screens, which offer additional features catering to specific requirements in medical settings. These screen protectors ensure the longevity and clarity of medical display screens, enhancing the overall viewing experience for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
