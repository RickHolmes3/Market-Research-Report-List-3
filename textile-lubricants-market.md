<p><p>Textile lubricants play a crucial role in the textile industry by enhancing the performance and durability of machinery and fabrics. Spin finish lubricants are used during the spinning process to reduce friction and improve yarn quality. Coning oils are applied to yarn cones to prevent tangling and breakage during processing. Knitting oils are used in the knitting process to reduce friction and improve fabric quality. Greases are utilized for lubricating bearings and other machine parts, ensuring smooth operation and extended lifespan.</p></p>
