<p><p>The unmanned ground vehicles (UGV) market can be classified into three main types: tele-operated, autonomous, and semi-autonomous. Tele-operated UGVs are controlled remotely by a human operator using advanced communication technologies. Autonomous UGVs operate independently without any human intervention, relying on onboard sensors and artificial intelligence algorithms to navigate and perform tasks. Semi-autonomous UGVs combine the capabilities of both tele-operated and autonomous vehicles, allowing for human control when needed while also possessing autonomous features. These UGV types cater to various applications and requirements, providing flexibility and efficiency in different operational scenarios.</p></p>
