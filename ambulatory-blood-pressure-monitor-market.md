<p><p>The ambulatory blood pressure monitor (ABPM) market includes two types: ordinary ABPM and mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM is a traditional approach that involves patients wearing a cuff and a recorder, which measures blood pressure periodically. Mobile-based ABPM, on the other hand, utilizes smartphone applications or wearable devices that allow patients to track their blood pressure in real-time. Mobile-based ABPM provides convenience and flexibility, enabling patients to monitor their blood pressure anytime and anywhere, thus gaining popularity in the market.</p></p>
