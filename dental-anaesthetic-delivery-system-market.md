<p><p>The market for dental anaesthetic delivery systems can be categorized into three main types. First, computer-controlled local anaesthetic delivery systems use technology to precisely administer anaesthesia, increasing efficiency and patient comfort. Second, jet injectors use high-pressure streams to deliver anaesthesia without needles, reducing the fear of injections. Lastly, intra-osseous anaesthesia systems deliver anaesthesia directly into the bone, allowing for effective numbing of the teeth and surrounding tissues. These three types of dental anaesthetic delivery systems provide various options for dentists to ensure optimal anaesthesia delivery during dental procedures.</p></p>
