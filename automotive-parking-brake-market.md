<p><p>The Automotive parking brake market is divided into two main types: Drum Brake Type and Disc Brake Type. Drum brake type parking brakes consist of brake shoes that press against the inner surface of a rotating drum to stop the vehicle from moving. On the other hand, disc brake type parking brakes use calipers to squeeze brake pads against a disc to halt the vehicle. Both types offer reliable braking performance, but disc brake type parking brakes are often preferred for their efficiency and durability.</p></p>
