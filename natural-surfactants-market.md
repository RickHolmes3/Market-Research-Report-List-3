<p><strong>The Natural Surfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic Natural Surfactants</li><li>Nonionic Natural Surfactants</li><li>Cationic Natural Surfactants</li><li>Amphoteric Natural Surfactants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural surfactants are classified into four main types based on their ionic charge: anionic, nonionic, cationic, and amphoteric. Anionic natural surfactants have a negative charge and are commonly found in products like soaps and shampoos. Nonionic natural surfactants have no charge and are used in products for sensitive skin. Cationic natural surfactants have a positive charge and are often used in conditioning hair products. Amphoteric natural surfactants can act as both anionic and cationic depending on the pH of the product.</p></p>
