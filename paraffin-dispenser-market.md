<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Paraffin dispensers are available in two main types - fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic paraffin dispensers are equipped with advanced technology that allows for hands-free operation, precise temperature control, and programmable settings. These are commonly used in large medical facilities and research laboratories. On the other hand, semi-automatic paraffin dispensers require some manual input but still offer efficient melting and dispensing of paraffin. These are often found in smaller clinics and histology labs. Both types of dispensers offer convenience and consistency in paraffin handling processes.</p></p>
