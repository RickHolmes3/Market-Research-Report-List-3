<p><p>The electronic toothbrush market is primarily divided into two types: vibration motion and rotation-oscillation motion. Vibration motion toothbrushes use rapid back-and-forth movements to clean teeth, while rotation-oscillation motion toothbrushes have a round brush head that rotates and oscillates to achieve a thorough cleaning. Both types are designed to provide more effective and efficient cleaning compared to manual toothbrushes, leading to improved oral health and hygiene for users.</p></p>
