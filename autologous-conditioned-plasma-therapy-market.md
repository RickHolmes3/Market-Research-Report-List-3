<p><p>Autologous Conditioned Plasma Therapy refers to the use of a patient's own blood components to promote healing and tissue regeneration. The market for this therapy can be categorized into four types: Pure Platelet-Rich Plasma (P-PRP), which mainly contains concentrated platelets; Leukocyte- and Platelet-Rich Plasma (LPRP), which includes both platelets and white blood cells; Pure Platelet-Rich Fibrin (P-PRF), which focuses on the fibrin matrix; and Leukocyte- and Platelet-Rich Fibrin (L-PRF), involving both fibrin and white blood cells. These variations offer different therapeutic approaches to address specific medical conditions and accelerate recovery.</p></p>
