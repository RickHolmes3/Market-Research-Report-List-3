<p><p>Paper containerboard market can be classified into two main types - Kraftliner and Testliner. Kraftliner refers to high-quality virgin paperboard made from 100% bleached softwood fibers, mainly used in packaging applications requiring high strength and durability. On the other hand, Testliner is a lower grade paperboard made from a mix of virgin and recycled fibers, commonly used for corrugated packaging. Both Kraftliner and Testliner play crucial roles in the packaging industry, catering to different market segments based on their specific characteristics and applications.</p></p>
