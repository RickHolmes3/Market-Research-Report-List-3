<p><p>Electrically insulated shoes, also known as dielectric footwear, are designed to provide protection against the risks of electrical hazards in the workplace. These shoes are usually made from non-conductive materials such as rubber or leather and have an insulating sole to prevent electrical current from passing through to the wearer's feet. They are commonly used in industries such as construction, utilities, manufacturing, and electricians.</p><p>The electrically insulated shoes market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers of this market is the increasing emphasis on worker safety in various industries. The rising awareness about the importance of personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety regulations has led to the increased adoption of electrically insulated shoes to minimize the risk of electrocution.</p><p>Additionally, the growing construction and infrastructure activities globally are also driving the demand for electrically insulated shoes.
