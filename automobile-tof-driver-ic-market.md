<p><strong>The Automobile ToF Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li><li>Indirect ToF (dToF) Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct ToF (dToF) Driver ICs are designed for automotive applications where the Time-of-Flight (ToF) sensor is directly connected to the driver IC, providing accurate distance measurements without the need for additional components. Indirect ToF (dToF) Driver ICs, on the other hand, require external components such as transimpedance amplifiers or voltage regulators to connect the ToF sensor to the driver IC. Both types of ICs cater to the growing demand for distance sensing technology in the automotive industry, with each offering unique benefits and capabilities.</p></p>
