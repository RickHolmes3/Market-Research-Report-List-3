<p><p>The oilfield services market encompasses various types of services required in the oil and gas industry. Drilling services involve the extraction of oil or gas from deep underground reservoirs using drilling rigs. Workover and completion services involve the enhancement or restoration of existing wells. Production services focus on optimizing oil and gas production and maintaining the well's integrity. Processing and separation services involve refining and separating raw oil and gas components. Lastly, geophysical services use specialized techniques to analyze underground formations and assist in locating potential drilling sites.</p></p>
