<p><p>Airflow management products are essential for optimizing cooling efficiency in data centers. Blanking panels fill empty rack spaces, preventing hot air recirculation. Grommets seal openings around cables, effectively redirecting airflow. Air-filled kits create barriers to block unwanted air paths. Enhanced brush/top and bottom covers prevent air leakage. Air diverters redirect airflow to areas with higher equipment heat loads. Containment systems enclose hot and cold aisles, maximizing cooling effectiveness. High-flow doors enhance airflow through racks. Other products include floor grommets, cabinet baffles, and raised floor tiles, among others, that all contribute to efficient airflow management and cooling in data centers.</p></p>
