<p><p>The carbonated beverage processing machinery market includes various types of equipment such as sugar dissolvers, carbonation equipment, blenders & mixers, heat exchangers, silos, and filtration equipment. Sugar dissolvers are used to dissolve sugar in the beverage, while carbonation equipment adds carbon dioxide to create carbonation. Blenders & mixers are used for mixing ingredients, heat exchangers control the temperature of the beverage, silos store ingredients, and filtration equipment removes impurities. Each type of equipment plays a crucial role in the production of carbonated beverages.</p></p>
