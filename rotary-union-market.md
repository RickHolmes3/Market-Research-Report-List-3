<p><p>Rotary unions are used in various industries to transfer fluid, air, or gas from a stationary supply to a rotating machine. There are two main market types for rotary unions: Multi-Passage and Single-Passage. Multi-Passage rotary unions have multiple channels allowing for the transfer of different media simultaneously. Conversely, Single-Passage rotary unions only have one channel for fluid transfer. Both types are used in different applications based on the specific needs of the system, such as the number of media to be transferred and the required flow rates.</p></p>
