<p><p>The Pleural Biopsy Market consists of different types of biopsies performed on the pleura, which is the thin tissue lining surrounding the lungs. The three main types of pleural biopsies are Malignancy Biopsy, Granulomatous Biopsy, and Lupus Biopsy. Malignancy Biopsy involves removing a small tissue sample from the pleura for analysis to diagnose or rule out cancer. Granulomatous Biopsy is performed to detect granulomas, which are small inflammatory nodules, often indicating diseases like tuberculosis or sarcoidosis. Lupus Biopsy focuses on diagnosing or monitoring lupus, an autoimmune disease affecting various body systems.</p></p>
