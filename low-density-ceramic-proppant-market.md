<p><p>Low density ceramic proppant market can be divided into two types based on mesh sizes: Large Mesh and Small Mesh. Large Mesh proppants are used in high-permeability formations, providing enhanced fracture conductivity. They are typically larger in size and offer increased strength to withstand high closure stress. On the other hand, Small Mesh proppants are used in low-permeability formations, offering improved flow control and optimal proppant placement. They are smaller in size and provide better proppant pack stability.</p></p>
