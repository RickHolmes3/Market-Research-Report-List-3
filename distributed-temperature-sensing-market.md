<p><strong>The Distributed Temperature Sensing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Point</li><li>Multipoint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) market consists of two main types: Single Point and Multipoint. Single Point DTS systems are designed for measuring temperature at a specific location, providing accurate and detailed data for a single spot. On the other hand, Multipoint DTS systems enable temperature measurements at multiple points simultaneously. These systems use optical fibers to monitor temperature changes along their entire length. Multipoint DTS systems offer a broader coverage area, allowing for real-time and continuous temperature monitoring in various applications such as oil and gas, power distribution, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
