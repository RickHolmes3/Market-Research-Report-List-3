<p><ul><li>Non-Bitumen Synthetic</li><li>Asphalt-Saturated Felt</li><li>Rubberized Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roofing underlayment market types include non-bitumen synthetic, asphalt-saturated felt, and rubberized asphalt. Non-bitumen synthetic underlayments are manufactured from materials like polyethylene and polypropylene for enhanced durability and moisture resistance. Asphalt-saturated felt underlayments are commonly used due to their affordability and waterproofing capabilities. Rubberized asphalt underlayments offer superior protection against water infiltration and are ideal for high-performance applications. Each type of underlayment caters to different roofing needs, providing various benefits in terms of cost-effectiveness, durability, and overall performance.</p></p>
