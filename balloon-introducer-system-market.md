<p><p>The Balloon Introducer System is a medical device used in interventional cardiology procedures. The system consists of a sheath, a dilator, and a hemostasis valve, which provide smooth and controlled access to the blood vessels during the procedure.</p><p>The market for Balloon Introducer Systems is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. These systems combine the features of both balloon introducers and arterial sheaths, offering enhanced maneuverability and ease of use for physicians.
