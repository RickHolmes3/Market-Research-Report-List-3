<p><p>The Piezo Ceramic market can be categorized into three main types: Lead zinc titanates (PZT), Lead titanate (PT), and Lead magnesium niobate (PMN). PZT is known for its high electromechanical coupling coefficient, making it ideal for various applications such as sensors and actuators. PT is valued for its excellent piezoelectric properties, making it commonly used in medical devices and industrial equipment. PMN offers high sensitivity and frequency range, making it suitable for advanced ultrasound imaging and acoustic devices.</p></p>
