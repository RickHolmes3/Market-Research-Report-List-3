<p><p>Cosmetic Grade Spherical Silica is available in three main types based on pore volume: medium pore volume, high pore volume, and non-porous silica. Medium pore volume silica has an intermediate level of porosity, allowing for moderate absorption and smooth texture in cosmetic products. High pore volume silica has a higher porosity, making it ideal for products that require high absorption and mattifying properties. Non-porous silica, on the other hand, does not have any pore volume and is used for its light-diffusing and soft-focus effects in cosmetics.</p></p>
