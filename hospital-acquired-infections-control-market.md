<p><p>Hospital Acquired Infections (HAIs) are infections that patients acquire while receiving treatment for medical or surgical conditions within a healthcare setting. The global market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the major trends in the Hospital Acquired Infections Control Market is the adoption of advanced technology for infection control.
