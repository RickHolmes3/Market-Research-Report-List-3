<p><p>Online membership software is available in two main types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based membership software is hosted on servers maintained by the software provider and accessed via the internet. It allows users to store data and access the software from anywhere with an internet connection. Web-based membership software, on the other hand, is installed directly on the user's own server and managed locally. Both types offer similar functionalities and benefits, such as managing memberships, handling payments, and automating administrative tasks, but differ in terms of hosting and accessibility.</p></p>
