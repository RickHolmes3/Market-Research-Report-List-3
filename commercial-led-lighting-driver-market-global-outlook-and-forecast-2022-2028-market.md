<p><p>The global commercial LED lighting driver market, forecasted from 2022 to 2028, includes various market types. DALI, or Digital Addressable Lighting Interface, is a popular type that allows for individual control of each LED light. 0-10V dimming enables users to adjust the brightness of LED lights using a low voltage signal. Standard (non-dim) drivers provide constant output without dimming capabilities. Triac dimming uses a triac switch to regulate light intensity. Lastly, smart drivers offer advanced features like wireless connectivity and automation for controlling LED lighting systems.</p></p>
