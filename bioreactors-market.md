<p><p>Bioreactors are classified into two main market types: disposable and reusable. Disposable bioreactors are designed for single-use applications and are discarded after each use. They offer advantages such as reduced cross-contamination risks and simplified operations. On the other hand, reusable bioreactors can be sterilized and used multiple times, making them suitable for long-term operations. These bioreactors provide cost benefits over time but may present challenges in terms of cleaning and sterilization procedures. Both types play a crucial role in bioprocessing, providing options to meet specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
