<p><p>Cloud Engineering Market Types refer to the different service models offered in the cloud computing industry. Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) provides virtualized computing resources such as servers and storage, allowing users to manage and control their own infrastructure. Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) offers a platform for developing, testing, and deploying applications without worrying about the underlying infrastructure. Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) provides access to software applications that are hosted and managed by a cloud provider, eliminating the need for installation and maintenance. These market types offer businesses flexibility, scalability, and cost efficiency in their IT operations.</p></p>
