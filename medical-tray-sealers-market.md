<p><p>Medical tray sealers are used in healthcare facilities to create a secure and sterile seal on medical trays, ensuring the safety and integrity of medical devices and supplies. Manual medical tray sealers require operators to manually load and seal trays, making them the most basic type. Semi-automatic tray sealers have automated sealing functions, while operators load the trays. Fully automatic tray sealers offer complete automation, with robotic loading and sealing processes. These different types cater to varying facility needs, offering different levels of efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
