<p><p>Paper laminates are widely used materials in the furniture and construction industries. Low pressure paper laminates are created by applying pressure under 100 bars, resulting in a more cost-effective option suitable for laminate flooring. On the other hand, high pressure paper laminates are manufactured by applying pressure above 100 bars, creating a stronger and more durable material ideal for kitchen countertops and wall panels. Both types of paper laminates offer different strengths and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
