<p><p>The automobile plate market consists of three main types: hot rolled steel plate, cold rolled plate, and coated steel plate. Hot rolled steel plates are made by passing a slab through a series of rollers, while cold rolled plates are created by further processing hot rolled plates. Coated steel plates have a layer of protective coating to enhance their durability and corrosion resistance. Each type serves different purposes in the automotive industry, with hot rolled plates being used for structural components, cold rolled plates for precise parts, and coated plates for added protection.</p></p>
