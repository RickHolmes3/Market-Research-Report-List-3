<p><p>Plastic pre-filled syringes market types include COP (cyclic olefin polymer), COC (cyclic olefin copolymer), PP (polypropylene), and other materials. COP pre-filled syringes offer high heat resistance and chemical inertness, making them suitable for sensitive drug formulations. COC syringes are known for their clarity and low levels of extractables. PP pre-filled syringes are cost-effective and commonly used for vaccines and insulin. Other materials may include PET or PBT, each offering specific benefits in terms of compatibility with different drugs and ease of sterilization.</p></p>
