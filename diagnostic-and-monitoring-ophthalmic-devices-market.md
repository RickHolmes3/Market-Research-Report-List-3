<p><p>The diagnostic and monitoring ophthalmic devices market includes various types of instruments used for assessing and monitoring eye health. Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems are used to visualize internal eye structures. Optical coherence tomographers provide detailed cross-sectional images of the eye's layers. Fundus cameras capture high-resolution images of the back of the eye. Tonometers measure intraocular pressure to detect glaucoma. Ophthalmoscopes examine the inside of the eye for abnormalities. Slit lamps are used for detailed examination of the anterior eye structures. Other devices in this market include visual field analyzers, corneal topographers, and pachymeters.</p></p>
