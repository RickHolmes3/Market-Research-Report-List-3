<p><p>Color detection sensors are used to detect and identify colors in various applications. Luminescence sensors detect colors based on their ability to emit light. RGB sensors use red, green, and blue light to detect and differentiate colors. Color sensors analyze the reflected light from an object to identify its color. Brightness sensors measure the intensity of light to determine the color shade. Contrast sensors detect color variations based on the contrast between background and foreground colors. These sensors find application in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
