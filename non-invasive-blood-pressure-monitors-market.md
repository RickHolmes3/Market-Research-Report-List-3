<p><p>Non-invasive blood pressure monitors are available in two main types- digital and mechanical. Digital blood pressure monitors use electronic pressure sensors to measure and display blood pressure readings on a screen. They are user-friendly and easy to use. On the other hand, mechanical blood pressure monitors require manual operation using a stethoscope and inflatable arm cuff. They are typically used by healthcare professionals in clinical settings. Both types of monitors provide accurate and reliable blood pressure measurements without the need for invasive procedures.</p></p>
