<p><ul><li>Pusher-Type Continuous Furnaces</li><li>Walking-Beam Continuous Furnaces</li><li>Roller Type Heat Treatment Furnace</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous working furnaces are used in various industries for continuous heat treatment of materials. The market types include pusher-type continuous furnaces, which use a pusher mechanism to move materials through the furnace for uniform heating. Walking-beam continuous furnaces have a walking beam system that moves materials in and out of the furnace. Roller type heat treatment furnaces use rollers to transfer materials through the heating process. Other markets may include belt furnaces, rotary hearth furnaces, and mesh belt furnaces, each with unique features for specific applications.</p></p>
