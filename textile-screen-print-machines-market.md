<p><strong>The Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile screen print machines come in two main types: in-line and carousel. In-line machines are designed for continuous production and can be connected to other machines in a production line. They are commonly used in large-scale operations for high-speed printing. Carousel machines, on the other hand, are ideal for smaller businesses or print shops with limited space. These machines have multiple print stations arranged in a circular design, allowing for easy loading and unloading of garments. Both types of machines are popular choices in the textile screen printing industry.</p></p>
