<p><ul><li>Low-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch</li><li>Medium-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch</li><li>High-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coal-Tar Pitch is classified into three main types based on their softening points: Low-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch, Medium-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch, and High-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch. Low-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch has a lower softening point and is commonly used in the manufacturing of roofing and waterproofing materials. Medium-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch has a moderate softening point and is utilized in the aluminum smelting industry. High-Temperature Coal-Tar Pitch has the highest softening point and is predominantly used in the production of carbon anodes for the aluminum industry.</p></p>
