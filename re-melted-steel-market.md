<p><ul><li>Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) Process</li><li>Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR) Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Re-melted steel markets involve the processes of Electro Slag Remelting (ESR) and Vacuum Arc Remelting (VAR). ESR is used to refine and homogenize steel ingots by passing an electric current through a consumable electrode in a water-cooled copper mold. VAR is a process where a consumable electrode is melted in a vacuum chamber to produce a more uniform and higher-quality steel ingot. Both processes are used to produce high-quality specialty steel products for various industries.</p></p>
