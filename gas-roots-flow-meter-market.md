<p><p>A Gas Roots Flow Meter is a type of flow meter that measures the flow rate of gas by using two rotating positive displacement rotors. These rotors are shaped like figure-eight and create a seal between the inlet and outlet of the meter. As the gas flows through the meter, the rotors rotate at a constant speed and the volume of gas passing through is calculated based on the number of rotations.</p><p>The Gas Roots Flow Meter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period.
