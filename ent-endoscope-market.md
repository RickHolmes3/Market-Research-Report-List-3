<p><strong>The ENT Endoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Endoscopes</li><li>Flexible Endoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ENT endoscope market comprises two main types: rigid endoscopes and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes are straight and have a fixed shape, allowing for better control during examination or surgery. They are typically used for procedures that require a straight path, such as nasal and sinus examinations. Flexible endoscopes, on the other hand, have a flexible shaft that allows for easier navigation through curved pathways. This type is usually used for examinations or procedures that require accessing hard-to-reach areas, like the throat or larynx.</p></p>
