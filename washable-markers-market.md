<p><p>Washable markers are available in two main types: Basic washable markers and Super-washable markers. Basic washable markers are designed to be easily washed off from surfaces with water and mild soap. They are suitable for most coloring activities and are commonly used by children and hobbyists. Super-washable markers, on the other hand, offer advanced cleaning properties. They can be easily removed even from fabrics and are typically used in educational settings where mess-free coloring is desired. Both types provide a convenient and cleaner alternative to traditional markers.</p></p>
