<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Battery ECU Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized</li><li>Modular</li><li>Distributed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery ECU market can be classified into three main types - Centralized, Modular, and Distributed. In a Centralized market, all battery management functions are carried out by a single ECU, providing a cost-effective solution for smaller vehicles. Modular market involves separate ECUs controlling different functions, offering flexibility and scalability. In a Distributed market, individual cells or modules have their own ECUs, allowing for efficient monitoring and control, particularly in larger vehicles with complex battery systems.</p></p>
