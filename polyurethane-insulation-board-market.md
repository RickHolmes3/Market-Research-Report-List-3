<p><p>Polyurethane insulation boards come in two main types: Expanded Polystyrene Foam and Extruded Polystyrene Foam. Expanded Polystyrene Foam boards are made by expanding polystyrene beads, creating a lightweight and cost-effective option for insulation. Extruded Polystyrene Foam boards are denser and more rigid, offering higher strength and insulation properties. Both types of boards are popular choices for insulation in construction projects, with Expanded Polystyrene Foam being more commonly used in residential applications and Extruded Polystyrene Foam being favored for commercial and industrial projects.</p></p>
