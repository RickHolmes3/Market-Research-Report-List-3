<p><p>Ankle syndesmosis treatment devices market includes suture button fixation and screw fixation types. Suture button fixation involves the use of a button and non-absorbable suture to stabilize the syndesmosis joint, allowing for dynamic movement and promoting natural healing. Screw fixation, on the other hand, uses screws to rigidly stabilize the joint, providing more stability but limiting movement. Both types of devices are used in the treatment of ankle syndesmosis injuries, with each offering unique benefits depending on the severity of the injury.</p></p>
