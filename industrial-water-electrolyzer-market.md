<p><p>The industrial water electrolyzer market includes two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzer and PEM electrolyzer. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use a liquid alkaline solution as the electrolyte to split water into hydrogen and oxygen. On the other hand, PEM electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane to separate the hydrogen and oxygen during the electrolysis process. Both types of electrolyzers play a critical role in producing hydrogen for industrial applications such as fuel cells and energy storage.</p></p>
