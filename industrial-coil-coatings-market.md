<p><p>Industrial coil coatings are used to protect and enhance metal surfaces in various industries. The market is divided into three main types: Primer, Back Paint, and Topcoat. Primer coatings provide corrosion protection and promote adhesion between the metal substrate and subsequent layers. Back Paint coatings offer additional protection to the back side of the metal coil. Topcoat coatings provide the final color, durability, and weather resistance. Each type of coating serves a specific purpose in ensuring the longevity and appearance of metal surfaces.</p></p>
