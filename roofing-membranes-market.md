<p><p>Roofing membranes are widely categorized into three main types: Thermoplastic Membrane, Synthetic Rubber (Thermoset), and Modified Bitumen. Thermoplastic membranes are flexible and can be repeatedly softened when heated and hardened when cooled. Synthetic Rubber membranes are made from rubber compounds that are durable and resistant to UV rays. Modified Bitumen membranes are made from asphalt and have added modifiers for improved performance. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of durability, flexibility, and resistance to weather conditions.</p></p>
