<p><strong>The Purging Compounds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Purging Compounds</li><li>Chemical/Foaming Purging Compounds</li><li>Liquid Purging Compounds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Purging compounds are used in various industries to clean injection molding machines and extruders. Mechanical purging compounds involve abrasive materials that physically scrub the machine surfaces. Chemical/foaming purging compounds use a chemical reaction to remove residue. Liquid purging compounds are pre-blended solutions that flow through the machine to clean it. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific application and machine requirements, making them essential in maintaining efficiency and productivity in industrial settings.</p></p>
