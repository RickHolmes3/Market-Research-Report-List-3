<p><p>The geotextile market is divided into two main types: synthetic and natural. Synthetic geotextiles are made from materials such as polyester, polypropylene, and polyethylene, providing durability, strength, and resistance to environmental factors. Natural geotextiles, on the other hand, are made from biodegradable materials like jute and coir, offering a more environmentally friendly option for erosion control and soil stabilization. Both types of geotextiles serve important functions in various construction and civil engineering projects, providing solutions for different needs and preferences.</p></p>
