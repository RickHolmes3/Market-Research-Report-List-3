Manual cleaning equipment involves manual techniques such as scrubbing or brushing to clean the devices. Automatic cleaning equipment utilizes automated processes for cleaning medical devices. Disinfection equipment is used to eliminate pathogens and microorganisms from the devices. These different types of equipment play crucial roles in ensuring the cleanliness and safety of medical devices.</p></p>
