<p><p>Fixed optical mounts are commonly categorized into two main types: cage mountable and post mountable. Cage mountable mounts are designed to be compatible with cage systems, allowing for easy integration into existing setups. Post mountable mounts, on the other hand, are designed to be attached directly to optical posts or other fixed structures. Each type offers its own advantages and may be preferred based on the specific requirements of the application, such as stability, precision, and ease of adjustment.</p></p>
