<p><p>Consumer Electronics Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) market is divided into two main types - Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM uses a toggle switch to store data, offering high speed and endurance. On the other hand, STT-MRAM uses spin-transfer torque to flip the magnetic orientation of the memory cell, providing lower power consumption and scalability. Both types are gaining popularity in consumer electronics for their fast performance, non-volatility, and high reliability.</p></p>
