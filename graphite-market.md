<p><p>Graphite markets can be categorized into two main types: Natural Graphite and Synthetic Graphite. Natural Graphite is derived from natural deposits and is typically used for applications requiring high purity and conductivity, such as in batteries and lubricants. Synthetic Graphite, on the other hand, is produced through high temperature processes and is used in industries where specific properties like thermal conductivity and strength are required, such as in the manufacturing of electrodes and refractories. Both markets cater to different needs and demand in various industries.</p></p>
