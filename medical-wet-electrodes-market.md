<p><p>Medical wet electrodes are a crucial component used in various medical procedures to monitor and record electrical signals from the body. There are two types of market segments for these electrodes, namely disposable and reusable. Disposable electrodes are cost-effective, single-use devices that offer hygienic advantages, reducing the risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, reusable electrodes can be sterilized after each use and are more economically sustainable in the long run. Both types serve the same purpose of accurately detecting and transmitting electrical signals, but differ in terms of cost and reusability.</p></p>
