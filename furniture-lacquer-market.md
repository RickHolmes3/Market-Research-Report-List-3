<p><p>Furniture lacquer is divided into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based lacquers contain chemical solvents that help the lacquer to dry quickly and create a hard and durable finish. On the other hand, water-based lacquers use water as a solvent, making them a more environmentally friendly option. Both types of lacquers offer different benefits and are used for various purposes in the furniture industry, catering to different preferences and requirements of consumers.</p></p>
