<p><p>The Rapid Antigen Testing market offers various types of tests to quickly detect the presence of specific antigens in biological samples. Immunosorbent assay, enzyme-linked immunosorbent, precipitation reaction, radioimmunoassay, agglutination reaction, and complement fixation are some types of tests available. Immunosorbent assay utilizes antibodies to capture and detect antigens, while enzyme-linked immunosorbent uses enzymes for detection. Precipitation reaction forms a visible precipitate when antigen and antibody combine. Radioimmunoassay measures radioactive compounds to detect antigens. Agglutination reaction causes clumping of particles when antigen and antibody react. Complement fixation measures the consumption of complement proteins during antigen-antibody reactions.</p></p>
