<p><p>Hydrogenated Diamond-Like Carbon Coating (DLC) can be applied through three main types of processes: Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD), Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD), and other methods. PVD involves evaporating and condensing a precursor material onto the surface, creating a thin DLC coating. PECVD uses a plasma to decompose a gas mixture and deposit DLC onto the substrate. Other methods may include arc-discharge deposition or pulsed laser deposition. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of coating quality, thickness, and application suitability.</p></p>
