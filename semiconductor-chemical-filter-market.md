<p><p>Semiconductor chemical filters are categorized based on the type of membrane used in the filtration process. These types include PP membrane, PES membrane, PTFE membrane, PE membrane, Nylon66 membrane, and others. Each type of membrane offers unique properties and benefits for filtering chemicals in the semiconductor industry. PP membranes are known for their chemical resistance, while PES membranes offer high flow rates. PTFE membranes provide excellent chemical compatibility, PE membranes have high thermal stability, and Nylon66 membranes are durable and long-lasting. Other types of membranes may offer specific advantages tailored to certain applications in semiconductor chemical filtration.</p></p>
