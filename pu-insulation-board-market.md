<p><p>PU insulation boards are available in three main types: lap type, socket type, and compound type. Lap type boards interlock with each other by overlapping edges, providing a seamless finish. Socket type boards feature a tongue and groove system for easy assembly and improved insulation properties. Compound type boards combine various elements to enhance thermal performance and structural integrity. Each type offers unique advantages and is tailored to different insulation needs, making them suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
