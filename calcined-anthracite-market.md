<p><strong>The Calcined Anthracite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Calcined Anthracite</li><li>Gas Calcined Anthracite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Calcined Anthracite is divided into two main types: Electrically Calcined Anthracite and Gas Calcined Anthracite. Electrically Calcined Anthracite is produced using high temperature electric furnaces, resulting in a high purity product suitable for use in steel and iron industries. Gas Calcined Anthracite, on the other hand, is produced by heating anthracite coal in gas-fired calciners. This type is commonly used in the production of carbon electrodes and cathode blocks. Both types play a vital role in various industrial applications due to their high carbon content and low ash content.</p></p>
