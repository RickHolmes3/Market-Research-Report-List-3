<p><p>The Oxygen Barrier PEX Pipe market includes three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa pipes are created through a process called peroxide cross-linking and offer excellent chemical resistance and high temperature capabilities. PE-Xb pipes are made through silane cross-linking and provide good dimensional stability and low-temperature impact strength. PE-Xc pipes are crafted using electron beam cross-linking and exhibit outstanding resistance to chlorine and heat aging. Each type has unique characteristics that suit different applications within the Oxygen Barrier PEX Pipe market.</p></p>
