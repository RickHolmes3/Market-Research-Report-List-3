<p><p>Auto Injectors Market Types include Single Fuel Injection (SFI), Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), SFI + GDI (combination of both), and Diesel Direct Injection (DDI). SFI injects fuel directly into the engine's intake manifold, while GDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber. SFI + GDI combines the benefits of both SFI and GDI technologies. DDI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber of diesel engines. Each type has its own unique features and advantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
