<p><p>In the online dermatology consultation market, there are two main types: asynchronous and synchronous. Asynchronous market refers to a platform where patients can upload their skin condition details, photos, and medical history at their convenience, and dermatologists review and provide diagnosis and treatment recommendations later. On the other hand, in a synchronous market, patients and dermatologists interact in real-time through video calls or chat platforms to discuss the skin issue, enabling immediate diagnosis and treatment guidance. These two types cater to different needs, offering flexibility and convenience in asynchronous market and quick real-time interactions in synchronous market.</p></p>
