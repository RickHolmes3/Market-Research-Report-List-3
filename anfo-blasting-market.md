<p><ul><li>Powdered Ammonium Oil Explosive</li><li>Porous Granular Ammonium Oil Explosive</li><li>Heavy Ammonium Oil Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ANFO blasting market includes different types of explosives such as Powdered Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil (PANFO), Porous Granular Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil (PGANFO), and Heavy ANFO. PANFO is a fine-grained explosive suitable for use in small diameter boreholes. PGANFO consists of porous granules that enhance oxygen diffusion, making it ideal for larger diameter boreholes. Heavy ANFO contains extra density-enhancing additives for use in challenging blasting conditions. Each type of ANFO explosive is tailored to meet specific requirements in the mining and construction industries.</p></p>
