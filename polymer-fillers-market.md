Organic fillers, like carbon black and natural fibers, enhance flexibility, impact resistance, and adhesion in polymers. Inorganic fillers are preferred for applications requiring high mechanical properties, while organic fillers are used when flexibility and impact resistance are essential. Both types of fillers play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of polymer materials in various industries.</p></p>
