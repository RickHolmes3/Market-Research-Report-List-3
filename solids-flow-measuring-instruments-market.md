<p><ul><li>Contacting Measuring Instruments</li><li>Contactless Measuring Instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solids flow measuring instruments can be classified into two main types: contacting measuring instruments and contactless measuring instruments. Contacting measuring instruments come in direct physical contact with the solid material to measure its flow, while contactless measuring instruments utilize non-contact methods such as lasers or microwaves to measure the flow of solids. Both types of instruments play important roles in industries that handle solid materials, providing critical data for process control and optimization.</p></p>
