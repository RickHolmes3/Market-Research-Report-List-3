<p><strong>The Syphilis Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Syphilis</li><li>Secondary Syphilis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Syphilis testing market comprises two main types - primary syphilis and secondary syphilis testing. Primary syphilis testing is conducted during the initial stage of the disease, typically around three weeks after exposure. It helps to identify the presence of Treponema pallidum bacteria in the body. On the other hand, secondary syphilis testing is performed during the second stage of the disease, which occurs a few weeks after the primary stage. This test helps to detect the antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the infection.</p></p>
