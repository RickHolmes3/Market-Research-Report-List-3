Dispersing agents help disperse the dye evenly in the solution, sequestering agents prevent metal ions from interfering with the dyeing process, scouring agents remove impurities from the fabric, wetting agents help the fabric absorb the dye more effectively, and other auxiliaries serve various purposes to improve the dyeing results.</p></p>
