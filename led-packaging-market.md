<p><strong>The LED Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Packaging</li><li>COB Packaging</li><li>CSP Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Packaging market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. SMD Packaging: Surface Mounted Device packaging is the most commonly used type, where LEDs are mounted directly onto a printed circuit board, providing high efficiency and compact size.</p><p>2. COB Packaging: Chip on Board packaging involves mounting multiple LED chips directly onto a single substrate, offering higher power density and better thermal management.</p><p>3. CSP Packaging: Chip Scale Package packaging is the latest technology, where the LED chip is directly mounted onto the PCB without a traditional package, providing even smaller size and better performance.</p></p>
