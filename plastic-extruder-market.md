<p><ul><li>Plunger</li><li>Single-screw</li><li>Double-screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic extruder market can be divided into four main types: Plunger, Single-screw, Double-screw, and Multi-screw extruders. Plunger extruders are most commonly used for short production runs and prototyping. Single-screw extruders are versatile and cost-effective, making them popular for a wide range of applications. Double-screw extruders offer better mixing capabilities and are often used for materials that require intense mixing. Multi-screw extruders, such as twin-screw or triple-screw, are used for high-output, high-viscosity materials that require precise control over the mixing process.</p></p>
