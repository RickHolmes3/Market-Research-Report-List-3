<p><p>Automotive exhaust analyzers are devices used to measure and analyze the components of vehicle exhaust gases. There are several types of exhaust analyzers available in the market, including non-dispersive infrared analyzers, flame ionization detectors, and chemiluminescence analyzers. Non-dispersive infrared analyzers measure gases based on their absorption of infrared light, while flame ionization detectors detect hydrocarbons. Chemiluminescence analyzers measure emissions through chemical reactions that produce light. Other types of exhaust analyzers may utilize different technologies to analyze exhaust gases for various pollutants.</p></p>
