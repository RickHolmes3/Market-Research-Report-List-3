<p><p>Manganese dioxide is commonly used in the battery industry and is available in three main market types - Electrolytic Manganese Dioxide (EMD), Natural Manganese Dioxide (NMD), and Chemical Manganese Dioxide (CMD). EMD is known for its high purity and is used in lithium-ion batteries for applications like electric vehicles. NMD is derived from natural sources and is used in alkaline batteries. CMD is a chemically synthesized form of manganese dioxide used in applications like water treatment and ceramics. Each type has specific properties that make them suitable for different applications.</p></p>
