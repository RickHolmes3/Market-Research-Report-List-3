<p><p>Auto Transformer Rectifier Units (ATRUs) can be classified into three main types based on the number of pulsations they produce: 6 Pulse, 12 Pulse, and 18 Pulse. </p><p>In 6 Pulse ATRUs, the rectification process is achieved using six thyristors, resulting in a relatively simple and cost-effective solution. </p><p>12 Pulse ATRUs employ twelve thyristors to reduce harmonics and improve power quality, making them suitable for applications requiring higher efficiency.</p><p>18 Pulse ATRUs use eighteen thyristors to further minimize harmonics and provide even cleaner and stable output power for sensitive equipment.</p></p>
