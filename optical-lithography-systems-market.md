<p><p>Optical lithography systems are used in the semiconductor industry for printing patterns onto silicon wafers. The different types of optical lithography systems are DUV (Deep UltraViolet) lithography systems, I-Line lithography systems, and others. DUV lithography systems use shorter wavelengths of light to achieve higher resolution and are widely used for advanced chip manufacturing. I-Line lithography systems use longer wavelengths and offer moderate resolution. Other types of lithography systems include E-Beam lithography, which uses electron beams, and X-Ray lithography, which utilizes X-rays. These systems cater to different manufacturing needs and enable the production of various semiconductor devices.</p></p>
