<p><p>Aluminium powder is a fine granular powder made up of small aluminum particles. It is commonly used in manufacturing processes such as powder coating, rocket fuel, and explosives. Aluminium pastes, on the other hand, are a mixture of aluminum powder and a solvent or carrier material. They are used in industries like automotive, construction, and printing for applications such as coatings and inks. Aluminium pigments are similar to pastes but are usually in a flake or powder form and are used for their reflective properties in industries like paints, coatings, and plastics.</p></p>
