<p><strong>The Anti-Creasing Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li><li>Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-creasing agents in the market are classified into two main types: Dye-Bath Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent and Wet Processing Lubricant/Anti-Creasing Agent. Dye-Bath Lubricants are added to the dyeing process to prevent creases and wrinkles in the fabric, while also improving color penetration. Wet processing lubricants are used in the finishing process to reduce friction between fabrics, preventing creases and improving overall fabric quality. Both types of anti-creasing agents contribute to enhancing textile quality and appearance.</p></p>
