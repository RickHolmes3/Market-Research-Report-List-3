<p><strong>The Medical Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polytetrafluoroethylene</li><li>Polyvinylidene Fluoride</li><li>Polypropylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical membranes market consists of three main types: Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF), and Polypropylene. PTFE is a synthetic fluoropolymer known for its excellent chemical resistance and low friction properties, making it suitable for medical applications such as drug delivery systems and implants. PVDF is a thermoplastic fluoropolymer known for its high chemical resistance and durability, making it commonly used in medical filtration and separation applications. Polypropylene is a thermoplastic polymer known for its versatility and low cost, making it widely used in medical devices and packaging applications.</p></p>
