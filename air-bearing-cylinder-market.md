<p><ul><li>Single Action</li><li>Double Action</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single action air bearing cylinder has air supply to only one side of the piston, allowing it to move in one direction only. This type is commonly used in applications where only one direction of motion is required. On the other hand, double action air bearing cylinder has air supply to both sides of the piston, allowing it to move in both directions. This type is more versatile and is used in applications where bi-directional motion is needed. Both types are essential in various industries for precise and controlled movements.</p></p>
