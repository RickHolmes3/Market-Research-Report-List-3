<p><p>A Gas Turbine Combined Cycle Power Plant is a type of power plant that utilizes both gas turbines and steam turbines to generate electricity. The gas turbine burns natural gas to produce electricity, and the waste heat produced is used to create steam, which is then used to drive a steam turbine to generate additional electricity. This combined cycle process increases the overall efficiency of the power plant, making it a more cost-effective and environmentally friendly option compared to traditional power plants.</p><p>The Gas Turbine Combined Cycle Power Plants Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.2% during the forecast period.
