<p><p>Vitrified Clay Pipes market offers two main types - Open Trench Socket Pipes and Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes. Open Trench Socket Pipes are installed using traditional trench excavation methods, providing a cost-effective and durable solution for wastewater systems. On the other hand, Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes are installed using remote-controlled tunneling machines, making them ideal for projects where minimal disruption and precise installation are required. Both types of pipes offer reliable performance and long-term durability in underground applications.</p></p>
