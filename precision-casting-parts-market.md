<p><p>Precision casting parts market can be divided into two main types: Silica Sol Casting and Water Glass Casting. Silica Sol Casting involves using a mold made from silica sol to create intricate and detailed parts with high dimensional accuracy. On the other hand, Water Glass Casting uses a mold made from water glass to produce parts with a smooth surface finish and good mechanical properties. Both types of casting processes are popular in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and machinery for their ability to produce high-quality parts.</p></p>
