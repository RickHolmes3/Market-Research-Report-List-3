<p><p>The corrugating paperboard market includes various types of paperboard based on the number of layers or walls they have. Single wall paperboard consists of one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two flat layers. Double wall paperboard has two layers of fluted paper and three flat layers. Triple wall paperboard contains three layers of fluted paper and four flat layers. Other types of paperboard may have different combinations of fluted and flat layers to meet specific packaging needs in the market.</p></p>
