<p><p>Aquaculture water treatment systems are used in the aquaculture industry to maintain and improve water quality in fish and shellfish farming. These systems help in controlling parameters such as dissolved oxygen levels, pH levels, ammonia, and nitrite concentration, and remove excess organic matter and other contaminants from the water. The main goal of these systems is to provide a controlled and optimal environment for aquaculture species, ensuring their health and growth.</p><p>Recirculating aquaculture systems (RAS) are a type of aquaculture water treatment system that allows for the reuse of water in fish or shellfish farms. In RAS, water is treated and recycled through a filtration system, reducing the need for large volumes of fresh water. This helps in conserving water resources and reducing the environmental impact of aquaculture operations.</p><p>The market for aquaculture water treatment systems and RAS is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
