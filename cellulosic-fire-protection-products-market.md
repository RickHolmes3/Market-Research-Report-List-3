<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellulosic fire protection products are available in two main market types: water-borne and solvent-borne. Water-borne products contain water as the main solvent, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They are typically used in areas where fire safety is a top priority. Solvent-borne products, on the other hand, contain solvents such as acetone or toluene. While they may be more durable and resistant to wear, they can be harmful to the environment and human health if not handled properly.</p></p>
