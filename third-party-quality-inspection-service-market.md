<p><p>Third Party Quality Inspection Service Market consists of four main types: </p><p>1. Pre-Production Inspection (PPI) focuses on quality control checks before mass production begins.</p><p>2. During Production Inspection (DPI) ensures that production is meeting the agreed-upon quality standards.</p><p>3. Pre-shipment Inspection (PSI) takes place after production is completed but before shipment to verify the goods' quality.</p><p>4. Loading Supervision (LS) involves monitoring the loading process to ensure that the correct quantity and quality of goods are being shipped.</p></p>
