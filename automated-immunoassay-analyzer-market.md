<p><p>Automated immunoassay analyzers are used in various applications such as chemiluminescence analysis, fluorescent immunoassay, radioimmunoassay, enzyme immunoassay, and others. Chemiluminescence analysis involves the detection of light emitted during a chemical reaction, while fluorescent immunoassay uses fluorescent markers to detect the presence of antigens. Radioimmunoassay measures the levels of antigens or antibodies using radioactive isotopes. Enzyme immunoassay utilizes enzymes to generate a detectable signal. The "others" category includes alternative methods like turbidimetry and nephelometry. These technologies help in the precise measurement of analytes in clinical, research, and pharmaceutical settings.</p></p>
