<p><p>Industrial bioprocessing refers to the production of various products using biological materials and processes. The market can be divided into two main types: upstream bioprocessing and downstream bioprocessing. Upstream bioprocessing involves the cultivation of microorganisms or cells, such as through fermentation, to produce desired biological materials. Downstream bioprocessing focuses on the purification and separation of these materials to obtain the final product. Both upstream and downstream bioprocessing are integral parts of the industrial bioprocessing market, contributing to the efficient and sustainable production of a wide range of products.</p></p>
