<p><p>Truck financial leasing is a form of financing in which a lessee (usually a business) can obtain the use of a truck or a fleet of trucks for a fixed period by making regular payments to the lessor. The increasing demand for trucks in various industries such as logistics, construction, and e-commerce has been a major driver for the market.
