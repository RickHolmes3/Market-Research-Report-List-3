<p><strong>The Functional Glass Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrolytic Coating</li><li>Sputtered Coating</li><li>Screen Coating</li><li>Spray Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional glass coatings market includes various types such as Pyrolytic Coating, Sputtered Coating, Screen Coating, Spray Coating and Others. Pyrolytic coating is deposited on glass during the float glass manufacturing process, while sputtered coating is created by bombarding target material with high-energy ions. Screen coating involves using a screen to apply a thin layer of material onto the glass, and spray coating utilizes a spray gun to spray a coating onto the glass surface. Other types of coatings include anti-reflective and self-cleaning coatings.</p></p>
