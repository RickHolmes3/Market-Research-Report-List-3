<p><p>The multi-parameter patient monitor market consists of three types: high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors. High-acuity monitors are advanced devices used in intensive care units and emergency settings to monitor vital signs and detect critical conditions. Mid-acuity monitors are designed for general hospital use, providing real-time data on patients' blood pressure, heart rate, and oxygen levels. Low-acuity monitors are portable, compact devices utilized in outpatient settings and home healthcare, primarily for continuously monitoring basic vital signs. These market types cater to different healthcare settings and patient needs.</p></p>
