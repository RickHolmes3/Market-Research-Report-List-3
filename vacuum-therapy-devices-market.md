<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure Wound Therapy Devices</li><li>Vacuum Constriction Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum therapy devices can be categorized into two main types: negative pressure wound therapy (NPWT) devices and vacuum constriction devices (VCDs). NPWT devices are used for wound healing by applying controlled negative pressure to the wound bed, promoting blood flow, removing excess fluid, and facilitating tissue regeneration. On the other hand, VCDs are primarily used in the treatment of erectile dysfunction. These devices create a vacuum around the penis, improving blood flow and helping achieve and maintain an erection.</p></p>
