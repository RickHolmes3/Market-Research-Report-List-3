<p><strong>The Industrial Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li><li>Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial Ultrasonic Position Sensor Market consists of two main types: Discrete Ultrasonic Position Sensor and Continuous Ultrasonic Position Sensor. Discrete sensors provide binary output based on a specific distance threshold, making them suitable for detecting objects at fixed distances. Continuous sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous output proportional to the distance between the sensor and the object, enabling precise distance measurement. These sensors find applications in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and robotics for position sensing and object detection purposes.</p></p>
