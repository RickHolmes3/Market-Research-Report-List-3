<p><p>Automotive Camera Monitoring System (CMS) includes two main types: Wireless Camera Monitoring System and Wired Camera Monitoring System. </p><p>Wireless CMS utilizes wireless technology to transmit video footage from cameras to a display screen inside the vehicle, providing real-time monitoring of the surroundings. This system offers flexibility and easy installation but may be susceptible to interference.</p><p>In contrast, Wired CMS connects cameras to the display screen via physical wires, ensuring a stable and reliable connection. However, the installation process may be more complex and time-consuming compared to wireless systems.</p></p>
