<p><strong>The Automotive Alarms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Car Alarm</li><li>Active Car Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive car alarms are designed to automatically activate when the vehicle is turned off and the ignition key is removed. These alarms rely on sensors to detect any unauthorized entry or movement of the vehicle. On the other hand, active car alarms require the driver to manually arm the system using a key fob or remote control. These alarms are usually more advanced and offer additional features such as remote start and GPS tracking. Both types of alarm systems serve the same purpose of deterring theft and protecting the vehicle.</p></p>
