<p><p>Folding bikes market is segmented into five types based on their folding mechanisms. Mid-fold bikes fold in the middle of the frame, making them compact for storage and transport. Vertical fold bikes fold vertically, allowing for easy carrying and storing in tight spaces. Triangle hinge bikes have a unique triangular hinge that allows for quick folding and unfolding. Magnet folding bikes use magnets to hold the frame together when folded. Suspension system bikes have a suspension system that absorbs shocks for a smoother ride.</p></p>
