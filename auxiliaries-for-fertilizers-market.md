<p><p>Auxiliaries for fertilizers market can be divided into five types: emulsifiers, fixing agents, dispersing agents, defoamers, and others. Emulsifiers help in mixing immiscible components, fixing agents ensure the stability of the fertilizer, dispersing agents help in even distribution of nutrients, defoamers prevent formation of foam during production, and other auxiliaries provide additional benefits such as increasing efficiency or shelf life. These different types of auxiliaries play crucial roles in enhancing the quality and effectiveness of fertilizers.</p></p>
