<p><p>DNA microarray chips are used to study gene expression and genetic variations. There are two main types of microarrays: oligonucleotide DNA microarrays (oDNA) and complementary DNA microarrays (cDNA). </p><p>oDNA microarrays use short and specific synthetic DNA fragments to represent genes. These arrays enable precise and sensitive analysis of gene expression levels.</p><p>On the other hand, cDNA microarrays use long DNA molecules called complementary DNA to represent genes. They are useful for studying the overall gene expression pattern in a biological sample.</p><p>Both types of microarrays have their unique advantages and applications in molecular biology research and diagnostics.</p></p>
