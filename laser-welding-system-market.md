<p><p>Laser welding systems can be classified into two main types: Solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines use solid crystalline materials to generate the laser beam, offering high power and precision for welding various materials. On the other hand, CO2 laser welding machines use a gas mixture to produce the laser beam, making them more suitable for welding non-metal materials. Both types of laser welding systems are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for their efficiency and accuracy in joining materials.</p></p>
