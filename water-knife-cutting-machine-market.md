<p><p>Water knife cutting machines are divided into two main types: sand water knives and pure water knives. Sand water knives use a mixture of water and abrasive particles to cut through hard materials like metal and stone. Pure water knives, on the other hand, use only high-pressure water to make precise cuts in softer materials like rubber and foam. Both types of machines are used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for their efficiency and versatility in cutting a wide range of materials.</p></p>
