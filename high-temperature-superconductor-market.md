<p><ul><li>1G HTS</li><li>2G HTS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>1G HTS (first generation high-temperature superconductor) market primarily consists of bismuth-based superconductors with critical temperatures under 40K. These superconductors require liquid nitrogen for cooling. On the other hand, 2G HTS (second generation high-temperature superconductor) market focuses on yttrium-based superconductors with critical temperatures above 80K, allowing for use of cheaper and more convenient cooling methods like liquid nitrogen. 2G HTS is considered to have better performance and is in high demand for various industrial applications.</p></p>
