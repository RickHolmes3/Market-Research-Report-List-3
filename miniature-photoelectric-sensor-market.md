<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Miniature Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Reflective Sensor</li><li>Retroreflective Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature photoelectric sensors are available in three main types - Through-Beam Sensor, Diffuse Reflective Sensor, and Retroreflective Sensor. Through-Beam Sensors consist of a transmitter and a receiver placed opposite each other, detecting objects that block the beam. Diffuse Reflective Sensors use the reflection of light off an object to detect its presence. Retroreflective Sensors work by bouncing light from a reflective target back to the sensor. Each type has its unique applications and advantages in various industries.</p></p>
