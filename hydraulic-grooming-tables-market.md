<p><p>Hydraulic grooming tables are available in two main market types: lifting type and rotating type. Lifting type tables use a hydraulic mechanism to raise or lower the table's height, allowing groomers to easily adjust it to their preferred working height. On the other hand, rotating type tables can rotate 360 degrees, enabling groomers to access any angle of the animal without the need for them to walk around the table. Both types offer convenience and functionality to groomers, enhancing their efficiency and allowing for a more comfortable grooming experience for the animals.</p></p>
