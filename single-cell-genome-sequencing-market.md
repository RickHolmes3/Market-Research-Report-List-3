<p><p>Single-cell genome sequencing market includes various types of technologies such as Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), quantitative PCR (qPCR), Microarray, and Multiple Displacement Amplification (MDA). NGS is a high-throughput approach, ideal for large-scale genomic analysis. PCR and qPCR are used for targeted sequencing of specific genes at high sensitivity. Microarray technology allows for simultaneous analysis of numerous genes. MDA is a method for amplifying small amounts of DNA for whole-genome sequencing. Each type of technology offers unique advantages for single-cell genome sequencing applications.</p></p>
