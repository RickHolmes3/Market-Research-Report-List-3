<p><strong>The Automotive EGR Gas Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Thermocouples Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive EGR Gas Temperature Sensor market includes two main types: Resistance Type and Thermocouple Type. Resistance Type sensors use a temperature-dependent resistor to measure the gas temperature, providing accurate readings within a specific temperature range. On the other hand, Thermocouple Type sensors rely on the temperature difference between two dissimilar metals to measure the gas temperature. Both types have their own advantages and limitations, allowing manufacturers to choose the best option based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
